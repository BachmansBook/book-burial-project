Welcome to the CI Book Project

This project gathers messages from influential people around the world, predicting what the world will look like in 100 years. The CI Book Project is a unique initiative that brings together visionary thoughts from various fields, including science, business, politics, and more. Each contributor provides insights and predictions about their area of expertise, offering a glimpse into the future.

Project Timeline

October 2025

Event description or milestone. This is the expected date for the burial of the CI Book in Central Park. The event will be attended by many of the co-authors, dignitaries, and the public. It will be a historic moment as we commit the book to the earth, to be preserved and revealed in 100 years.

November 2023

Event description or milestone. The project officially launches, and invitations are sent to influential figures around the world to contribute their messages and predictions for the future. This marks the beginning of the CI Book Project's journey.

January 2024

Event description or milestone. The first drafts of contributions are received and reviewed by the editorial team. This phase involves selecting the most impactful and insightful messages to be included in the book.

June 2024

Event description or milestone. The book enters the final stages of compilation. Editing, formatting, and design work are completed to ensure the book is a high-quality artifact worthy of its historic purpose.

September 2024

Event description or milestone. The completed book is presented to the public for the first time in a special unveiling event. This allows the contributors and the public to see the finished product before it is sealed for burial.
