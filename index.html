About the Project

This project involves burying a book in Central Park, New York City, to be revealed in 100 years. Our goal is to create a lasting legacy and connect with future generations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this project?

The purpose is to create a time capsule that connects current and future generations through the preservation of a book.

Where is the book buried?

The book is buried in a secure location in Central Park, New York City.

When will the book be revealed?

The book will be revealed 100 years from the date of burial.
