FAQ

What is the CI Book Project?

The CI Book Project is a century-long initiative to bury a book in Central Park, New York, to be revealed in 100 years.

How can I participate?

You can participate by contributing to the project through donations, volunteering, or sharing your stories.

What will the book contain?

The book will contain stories, predictions, and messages from the current generation to be discovered by future generations.

Where will the book be buried?

The book will be buried in a secret location within Central Park, New York.
